Instructions

This is IGC HK Exam - WJEC past paper bank, questions are related to school mock exams and are likely to come up in exams. There are three main documents for each subject, Past Paper Bank, Questions You Must Do and QER. The past paper bank consist of 90-100 pages so please do it at your own place and please don't complete all of them. As the second document title shows, these Questions You Must Do are questions style you are very likely to encounter during exams, so please spend time doing these. Lastly is the QER predictions, these are just sample QER. If you have any questions or concerns with copyright issues, please email wjec@igchkshop.dpdns.org for enquires or visit www.igchkshop.dpdns.org for further reference. Good luck with revision and may the force be with you!
